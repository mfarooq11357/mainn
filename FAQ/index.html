Frequently Asked Questions

What is your car rental agency? +

Our car rental agency offers a wide selection of vehicles for rent, including sedans, SUVs, and trucks, to meet your transportation needs for any occasion.

How much does it cost to rent a car? +

Rental costs vary based on the type of vehicle, rental duration, and additional options. Our prices start from $25 per day for economy cars. Please visit our pricing page for detailed information.

Where can I pick up and drop off the car? +

You can pick up and drop off your rental car at any of our convenient locations across the city. We also offer airport pickup and drop-off services. Check our locations page for more details.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. You can cancel your reservation for free up to 24 hours before the pickup time. For cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance, a small fee may apply.
